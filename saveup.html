SaveUp / Ewyzly Project

Overview

During my bachelor's degree at RMIT, I had the opportunity to work with Quake Tech Inc as work integrated learning, and it was truly a challenge for me. Being part of the development team, I was teamed up with fellow students in developing two projects. One being Save Up / Ewyzly which was a money saving finacial app.

So From this project what I learned was using Ionic and Angular to create Mobile Apps.

Problem

During this Project we faced many problems, One of the problem for us was that for this project we had to work closely with our client which was James at Quake Tech Inc. However James was in Chicago, USA. which is 15 hours behind melbourne where our team was, so we had that communication gap and we weren't able to contact James at one point as well.

Solution

So for that problem what we had to do was make sure we had a contact with James to discuss what we actually had to develop for this app as during that time Save Up or eWyzly was already launched in play store and app store.

So what we did was emailed James that we wanted to take the communication from emails to skype. So we could communicate and get replys quickers. From that quick thinking we were able to complete the project and our assignment as well.
