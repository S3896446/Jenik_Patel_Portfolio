Kiah Project

Overview

During my time at NewPathWeb, I had the amazing opportunity to work on the Kiah website, and it was truly a game-changer for me. Being part of the development team, I was heavily involved in ensuring that the website not only functioned flawlessly but was regularly updated as well. When implementing the regular updates, I put my skills in PHP, HTML, CSS, and wordpress to good use.

The best part was seeing the website come to life after deploying it, seeing it align perfectly as Kiah visioned it. The hard work and dedication paid off, and Kiah's success was a testament to the effort and dedication put in by the entire team. It was an incredibly fulfilling experience that showcased my ability to thrive and excel in challenging environments.

Problem

When I was working on the Kiah project at NewPathWeb, we faced a pretty tough challenge. The client wanted a new careers page integrated into their website, and it had to work seamlessly with their existing setup. As a newbie developer, I have to admit, I was a bit intimidated at first. I didn't have a ton of experience with PHP and WordPress, and this looked like a mountain to climb.

Solution

I have to admit I was a bit nervous – being a newbie developer and all. But I decided to tackle it head-on with a positive attitude. To bridge my knowledge gap in PHP and WordPress, I delved into research to figure out what needed to be done and how to do it right. I wasn't alone in this journey. I tapped into online resources, followed tutorials, and turned to my more experienced colleagues for guidance. Whenever I hit a roadblock, I didn't hesitate to ask for help from the project team, and together, we brainstormed and came up with solutions. It was a learning curve for sure, but I kept pushing forward with determination. And guess what?

I managed to pull it off – the careers page was up and running smoothly on the website, fitting in perfectly with the client's requirements. This whole experience not only boosted my tech skills but also gave me a big confidence boost and taught me the value of perseverance as a budding developer.
